Expensive Look & Budget

Invest in fewer pieces made of high-quality materials. Quality pieces often look more expensive. Choose neutral and timeless colors. Black, white, beige, and navy tend to look more sophisticated and can be easily mixed and matched.

Skincare Routine

Start your day by cleansing your face to remove any dirt or oil accumulated overnight. Use a gentle cleanser suitable for your skin type. Apply toner to balance the skin's pH levels and prepare it for the next steps. Choose a toner with ingredients suitable for your skin type.

Haircare Tips

Washing your hair regularly helps to remove dirt, excess oil, and product buildup. However, avoid over-washing as it can strip your hair of its natural oils. Stick to washing your hair 2-3 times a week for optimal results.
